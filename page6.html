Mini - Militia

THIS EVENT IS OPEN FOR ALL.

EVENT TYPE :
Single

REGISTRATION FEES :
 Rs. 30/- for Commando Mode
 Rs. 150/- for Army league


  •  Rules(Commando Mode):-:
  • ⦁ Match will be between 3 players.
  • ⦁ Every match will have a winner he will win Rs. 60 onspot.
  • ⦁ Everyone will be provided a fresh .apk file to play.
  • ⦁ Map to be played on will be decided by vote between players. Map getting most votes will be played.
  • ⦁ Players can re-register multiple times.
  • ⦁ Everyone has to play on Zero level with default guns.
  • ⦁ Each match will be of 5 mints. In case of tie we will have another match of 3 mints between the tied players.
  • ⦁ Cheating will ban a player from whole tournament.
  • ⦁ Player need to bring his/her android otherwise wouldn’t be able to participate.
  • ⦁ Using any mod also will lead to ban the player from the tournament.
  • For army League : 3 - members 
  • Game mode: Deathmatch (Lanwifi).
  •  Time: 6 mins 30 secs in league matches 10 min in semifinals and 15min in final
  •  A team or a player can register multiple times in this event.
  •  Coin Toss is used to decide which map is to be selected.
  •  In the incident of tie the other team gets to choose the map for rematch.
  •  Each team can choose a variety of weapons depending upon the map.
  •  If one of the players forfeits the match, the team will be disqualified and the other team will be declared winner(IRRESPECTIVE OF THE SCORE)
  •  The players will need to uninstall the game version that they have and need to install the genuine version provided by the organizer at the time of event. In case any player is found to play the game with hacked or crack version the entire team will be disqualified.
  • The Organizer has the right to disqualify and dismiss any registered player from the tournament, at their discretion, at any stage of the tournament, for any reason, with no prior warning to the player.
  •  Any protests or disagreements with tournament rulings must be made directly to the Organizer in a reasonable manner at the time of the incident, at which time a prompt decision will be made. The Organizer's decisions are final.
All the above rules should be followed without argument with the tournament official, the violation of which may result in player or team disqualification, the decision to do the same lies in the hand of the tournament official present there, rules can be changed at any point of time as decided by the event co-ordinator present there.
