CS-1.6

EVENT TYPE :
Team of 5 Members
REGISTRATION FEES :
 Rs 200/- per team

  • Total 8 teams are allowed. Teams will be registered based on First Come First Serve.
  • 5 members are allowed per team.
  • The players can bring their own mouse, keyboard, mousepad. If not, then the said accessories will be provided.
  • Total 20 rounds to be played. The team winning first 16 rounds will be considered as winners.
  • Overtime rounds will be played if the score ties.
  • Round Time: 1:55 mins
  • Starting Money: $800
  • Freeze Time : 15 secs
  • Buy Time : 20 secs
  •  Map Pool:- de_dust2,  de_cbble, de_mirage ,de_inferno ,de_nuke ,de_aztec,de_train
  •  Maps will be selected by usual Ban-Pick method.
  • No Cheats(Wall Hacks, Aimbot, etc) or Game Scripts(Bunny Hop, etc) are allowed.If found using them, then the whole team will be disqualified.
  • PRIZE MONEY: Rs 2500/-  and more goodies for all participants
  • Contact : Niladri Chandra : 8910871144
All the above rules should be followed without argument with the tournament official, the violation of which may result in player or team disqualification, the decision to do the same lies in the hand of the tournament official present there, rules can be changed at any point of time as decided by the event co-ordinator present there.
